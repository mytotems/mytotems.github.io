Privacy policy

1. Information collection & use

Website visitors

Like most website operators, NoCoffee SARL collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. NoCoffee SARL's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how NoCoffee SARL's visitors use its website.

NoCoffee SARL also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. NoCoffee SARL does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information.

NoCoffee SARL uses Plausible Analytics to track overall trends in the usage of our website. Plausible Analytics collects only aggregated information, which does not allow us to identify any visitor to our website. For more information, please visit the Plausible Analytics Data Policy.

Paid customers

The myTotems service available at mytotems.page requires a login in order to provide you with a personalized account. During our account registration process, we will collect personally identifiable information (login, name, email address, credit card number, billing address, phone number and optionally VAT number) and keep this information on file in our secure server in order to process your recurring invoices. This information is only used to verify credit card authenticity, and issue invoices.

After completing our registration form, you will receive a welcome email describing your new myTotems account, and how to access that account. Upon discontinuation, your information will be destroyed in our billing system. If we are unable to process your subscription for a given month, this information, along with your account information, may be used to contact you.

2. Communications from NoCoffee SARL

Service-related Announcements

We will send you service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance, we might send you an email. Generally, you may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account.

Other Messages

We may also send you messages from time to time about new features or services that we will offer to our users. You may opt-out of these communications at any time by clicking on the link at the bottom of our emails.

Customer Service

Upon issuing a customer support request to NoCoffee SARL / myTotems, we may use your information and email address to contact you regarding your inquiry.

3. Information sharing and disclosure

Aggregated statistics

NoCoffee SARL may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites Your identity and personal information will be kept anonymous in Aggregated statistics.

Third-party service providers

NoCoffee SARL employs third-party service providers to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include credit card processing, email service providers, analyzing data, providing customer service. These service providers have access to personal information needed to perform such functions on our behalf.

Change of ownership

In the event of a change of ownership or other business transition, such as a merger, acquisition or sale of our assets, your information may be transferred in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

4. Cookies and log files

NoCoffee SARL uses cookies and log files to track user information. A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Users who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using our websites.

5. Security of your Personal Information

No method of safeguarding information is 100% secure. NoCoffee SARL uses a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. NoCoffee SARL secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.

6. Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, NoCoffee SARL may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in its sole discretion. NoCoffee SARL encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

7. Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please send us a detailed message at contact@mytotems.page. We will make every effort to resolve your concerns.
